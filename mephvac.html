MEP/HVAC Division

The MEP/HVAC division undertakes HVAC projects, Electro-mechanical projects & services (EMPS) and plumbing work including BMS, Internal Electrification and Sub-station work meeting international standards. We have a wide range of experience in Package Ducted Split Chillers and Air Handling Units.


We design and install a full range of air conditioning projects including Air-cooled and Water-cooled chillers, Air Handling units, Fan Coil units, Ducted Splits, Floor Mounted packaged conditioners, Unitary type self-contained packaged air conditioners, High-wall splits, and window air conditioners.

Our clientele includes Industries, Banks, Hospitals, Office complexes, and Commercial complexes.


We also offer turnkey Electro-mechanical contracting and services in the country. We have completed projects for large air conditioning systems involving imported equipment manufactured to international standards. We ensure a superior quality of workmanship with advanced engineering capabilities. We have a full-fledged team of qualified engineers and technical staff in the Air-Conditioning and Electro-Mechanical divisions to carry out large turnkey projects.
