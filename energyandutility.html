Energy & Utility Division

UCTC is positioned to become a single point of contact for creating, managing & maintaining energy efficient and environmentally friendly utility plants. UCTC provides complete offerings to the end user from concept and commissioning stages to operation, maintenance and even manning plants and machinery.


UCTC has experienced professionals who have more than 20 years of experience in technical solutions and services. In-house project management capabilities extend to setting up energy, environment, utility, MEP, HVAC projects for customers. UCTC represents various principals whose resources are available full time in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to support UCTC locally.


Energy and Utility Solutions

  • Heating equipment - powered by a variety of fuels including HFO and Gas
  • Absorption Chillers - fired by fuels, waste heat, steam, or hot water from solar sources

  • Custom built, Explosion-proof A/C units, Air handlers, Chilled Beams, Server Rooms, and Chemical Lab speciality units
