Industrial Services Division

Industrial services division provides comprehensive coating services for heavy industries throughout the Kingdom. In support of its heavy industries, we have earned a reputation for excellence in coating and lining work with all Petrochemical, Heavy industries and Port Services.

This includes abrasive blasting and special coatings, fabrication, maintenance of industrial facilities, general civic, mechanical & electrical contracting.


Our major projects include Petrochemical plants, Refineries, Fertilizer manufacturing, Desalination plants, Tank forms and Industrial Ports. Our capabilities, resources and experience with a wide range of industrial cleaning make UCTC an outstanding choice for industries. We are dedicated to servicing out clients with innovative management, experienced teams and ever-reliable commitment.
